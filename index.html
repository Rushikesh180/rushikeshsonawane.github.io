I am a PhD researcher in high-energy astrophysics, working on Gamma-Ray Bursts (GRBs). My research combines spectro-polarimetry, spectral modeling, and numerical simulations to understand the most energetic explosions in the universe. I am an active member of the Fermi-GBM and AstroSat-CZTI collaborations.

  • Spectro-polarimetric studies of GRBs through the joint analysis of Fermi-GBM and AstroSat-CZTI, probing polarization signatures and jet structures.
  • Spectral modeling using Fermi-GBM and Fermi-LAT data to understand emission mechanisms and energy distribution.
  • Numerical simulations of GRB jets to study dynamics, emission regions, and jet geometry.
